NSSCO Grade 11 Tutorial Programme

Our flagship programme provides full-year, structured academic tutorials for Grade 11 learners working toward their NSSCO qualifications. As a DNEA-registered centre, all examinations written at Bells carry full official recognition. Classes are timetabled, curriculum-aligned, and delivered by qualified, experienced subject educators in modern, air-conditioned classrooms equipped with audio-visual technology.

Learners attend structured classes across their chosen subject combination, with access to past examination papers, continuous assessment, revision sessions, and full examination registration handled by the school.

Part-Time Tutorial Programme

For learners who cannot commit to full-time attendance, our part-time programme offers the same quality of instruction on a flexible schedule. Learners select the subjects they need and attend tutorials according to a timetable agreed with the school — making quality education accessible around work or family commitments.

This programme is particularly suited to working adults returning to education, school leavers re-sitting specific subjects, and NAMCOL students seeking additional structured support alongside their NAMCOL studies.

Examination Preparation Programme

Intensive preparation for upcoming NSSCO examinations. This programme concentrates on past-paper analysis, examination technique, content revision, and timed practice — available as a standalone short course in the months leading up to examinations. Suitable for private candidates and NAMCOL learners.

Students work through past examination papers under timed conditions, receive detailed feedback, and cover high-frequency examination topics systematically.

Subject Coaching

One-to-one or small-group coaching in specific subjects. Ideal for students performing well overall but needing to strengthen a particular area. Subject coaching is structured, measurable, and tied to clear learning objectives.

A coaching plan is developed at the outset, aligned to the student's current level and target outcomes, with regular progress checks throughout.

Examination Registration Services

As a registered NSSCO examination centre under the Ministry of Education's Directorate of National Examinations and Assessment (DNEA), Bells Private School offers full examination registration services. We handle the formal registration of candidates for national external NSSCO examinations on their behalf.

This service is available to our enrolled learners as well as external private candidates and NAMCOL students who need a registered centre to sit their examinations. Learners also gain access to past examination papers and revision resources throughout their registration period.

Holiday Classes

Productive academic sessions during school holiday periods. Our holiday classes help students consolidate what they have learned during term, address gaps in understanding, and prepare for the upcoming term's content.

Holiday classes are scheduled across key school break periods and are available for students across our full subject offering.
