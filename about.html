Our Story

Established to Serve
Academic Excellence

Bells Private School is a fully accredited educational institution located in Khomasdal, Windhoek. Since its establishment, the school has played a pivotal role in Namibia's private education sector by providing structured academic support to learners preparing for the Namibia Senior Secondary Certificate Ordinary Level (NSSCO) examinations. As a registered centre under the Ministry of Education's Directorate of National Examinations and Assessment (DNEA), we are an official NSSCO examination centre — one of the few private institutions in Windhoek with this standing.

We specialise in high-impact tutorial services for full-time and part-time learners — particularly those seeking to improve or upgrade their academic results. Our programmes serve school leavers re-sitting NSSCO subjects, learners who underperformed in public schools, adult learners, NAMCOL students, and private candidates seeking structured academic support.

"To become Namibia's leading private tutorial and examination centre, renowned for turning academic setbacks into success stories."

Our qualified, passionate educators deliver structured tutorials, examination preparation, holiday classes, and individual subject coaching. We also offer examination registration services and access to past papers — giving every learner the tools they need to succeed.

Our People

Teaching Staff & Leadership

The strength of Bells Private School lies in the quality and dedication of its teaching staff. The school is led by a professional team with extensive experience in education and administration. Staff members are carefully selected on the basis of their qualifications, experience, and commitment to learner development.

Ongoing training and performance monitoring ensure that a consistent standard of excellence is maintained across all areas of the school. Our leadership team ensures that Bells operates with academic rigour, administrative efficiency, and a culture of continuous improvement.

Looking ahead, the school's strategic goals include expanding to Grade 12 AS Level tutorials, introducing digital and virtual learning platforms, and strengthening partnerships with government bodies and private sector stakeholders.
