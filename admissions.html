How to Enrol

A Simple, Clear Process

Enrolment at Bells Private School is open throughout the year for most programmes. We serve school leavers re-sitting NSSCO subjects, learners who underperformed in public schools, adult learners, NAMCOL students, and private candidates. Our process is straightforward and transparent — so you can focus on what matters most: getting started.

Who We Serve

✓ School leavers re-sitting NSSCO subjects
✓ Learners who underperformed in public schools
✓ Adult learners returning to education
✓ NAMCOL students seeking extra support
✓ Private candidates needing a registered centre
✓ Working learners needing flexible schedules
1

Enquiry

Contact us by phone, email, or in person to discuss your academic needs, available programmes, and subject selection. We will advise on the most suitable option.

2

Registration

Complete the registration form and provide the required documentation. Confirm your programme choice and schedule. Registration fees are payable at this stage.

3

Start Classes

Attend your first session and begin your structured programme. Your timetable, subject teachers, and materials will all be in place from day one.

Admission Requirements

  • Certified copy of most recent school results or academic record
  • Certified copy of identity document or birth certificate
  • Two recent passport-size photographs
  • Completed registration form (available on request)
  • Proof of address (for school records)

Programme Selection Notes

  • Students should ideally be registered for Grade 11 or equivalent level
  • Subject combinations are discussed and agreed with an advisor at registration
  • Minimum subject load and attendance requirements apply per programme
  • Part-time and exam-prep enrolment is available year-round
  • Placement assessments may be conducted for certain subjects
  • NAMCOL students and private candidates are welcome — contact us for specific requirements

Fees & Payment

Programme fees vary depending on the number of subjects and programme type chosen. All fees are transparent and discussed upfront during the enquiry stage. We offer structured payment plans to support families in managing the cost of education.

Request Fee Schedule

Note: Fees are reviewed periodically. Current fee schedules are provided during the registration process or on request from our administrative office. No fees are paid prior to formal registration.
