About The Institution

A Foundation Built on Academic Discipline

Bells Private School is a fully accredited educational institution located in Khomasdal, Windhoek. Registered under the Ministry of Education's Directorate of National Examinations and Assessment (DNEA), we provide structured academic support to learners preparing for the Namibia Senior Secondary Certificate Ordinary Level (NSSCO) examinations.

We specialise in high-impact tutorial services for full-time and part-time learners — particularly those seeking to improve or upgrade their academic results. Our qualified, experienced educators and learner-centred approach have made us a trusted name in Namibia's private education sector.
